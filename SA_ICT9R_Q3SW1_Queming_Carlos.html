Synopsis:


Ginny and Georgia is a Netflix original series that follows the adventures of a young mother named Georgia and her daughter Ginny as they navigate the ups and downs of life in a small New England town. With a strong focus on family dynamics, the series explores themes of love, loss, and the complexities of mother-daughter relationships.

Characters:

Georgia Miller

Georgia Randolph (born Mary Rose Reilly, formerly known as Georgia Miller), is a 31-year-old female who lives in Wellsbury, Massachusetts. She is the mother of Ginny Miller and Austin Miller. She is portrayed by Brianne Howey, while her teenage counterpart is portrayed Nikki Roumel.
